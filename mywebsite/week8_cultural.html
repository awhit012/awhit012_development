Pairing

What its like

I thought I would be bad at working with someone, but I think I am good at it! I thought it would be very difficult to me to problem solve with another person because I assumed I would be too in my own head. It turns out having someone else there can help guide my thought and keep me from going off on a tangent or getting lost! In the context of DBC prep, it is great to see that other people are on the same page as me as far as progress.

What is fun

It is really fun to bounce ideas around! Its fun when I'm lost and my pair has an idea. Its fun when my pair is lost and I have an idea.

What is difficult

Communication is one of the most important and challenging things about being a human. I had one pair that went badly. I was exhausted and really not up for it, but I felt obligated to try my best. I should have communicated how I was feeling instead!

Feedback

How's it feel?

It feels great to get good positive feedback! It feels less than great to get negative feedback at first. But after a few breaths, if its helpful, it can be transformative!

Is it helpful?

Sometimes! Usually actually. Just for some reinfocement at the very least. At best an actionable piece of advice! One example that I recieved said that I should communicate better when I was thinking or looking something up so I'm not just dead air.

Whats it like to give feedback?

For me giving feedback is more difficult than recieving. I'm sure I will get better at it as I get more comfortable pairing. I think the barrier is that I tend to be more concerned with how I am doing and don't notice what my pair could be doing better.

Using pairing and feedback to guide learning

I think this is an excellent way to guide learning. Every time I have had anxiety about my level of understanding, after I pair that anxiety has gone away. I discover that someone else feels the same way, and dispite our anxiety we solve the problem together. Then the next time I feel less anxious. I'm getting to where I am feeling confident going in to our final week of prep, and pairing has a LOT to do with it.
